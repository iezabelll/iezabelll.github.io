WHAT
IS ART?

the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture, producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power. "the art of the Renaissance"






P A G E 2

HISTORY
ABOUT ART

Art history doesn't consist in simply listing all the art movements and placing them on a timeline. It is the study of objects of art considered within their time period. Art historians analyze visual arts' meaning (painting, sculpture, architecture) at the time they were created.
